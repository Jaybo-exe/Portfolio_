Hello My name is Jaden Poe

I’m a design student and photographer with a strong interest in visual storytelling, UI/UX, and art-driven experiences. My work explores the balance between structure and experimentation, combining my research, systems thinking, and creative intuition. With a background in athletics and photography, I bring a disciplined, detail-oriented approach to design while still being connected to the less structured part of art. I’m constantly learning and refining my craft, building work that feels intentional and creative.
